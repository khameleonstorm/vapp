Final Project from Creative Entrepreneurship class at Miami Ad School

Brief: New Brand from zero to vision:

Identify a market/industry: Choose a specific market or industry that you’re interested in, and research the current trends and challenges that businesses in that space are facing.

Define the main problems: Identify a problem or challenge that is prevalent in the market/industry you’ve chosen. This could be anything from a lack of innovative solutions to a specific pain point experienced by consumers.

Develop a brand: Create a brand that addresses the problem you’ve identified. This could involve designing a new product, service, or business model that solves the problem in a unique and creative way. Your brand should have a clear mission, values, and messaging that resonates with your target audience.

Main Problems In The Vape Industry

Teen Vaping

E-Waste

The use of e-cigarettes, has become a significant problem in recent years. According to the National Institute on Drug Abuse, in 2020, approximately 19.6% of high school students reported using e-cigarettes, with 4.7% using them daily.

 

Disposable vapes add to the growing problem of electronic waste and contain non-biodegradable materials and toxic chemicals that can harm the environment. Proper disposal and exploring environmentally-friendly alternatives are needed to reduce their impact.

Objective

To create a seamless buying experience for customers interested in purchasing vape products while also addressing the issues of Teen Vaping and E-waste in the industry.

By introducing vending machines that are linked with the VAPP app, we aim to simplify the purchase process and reduce the hassle for customers.

Furthermore, by providing a recycling container in the vending machines, we hope to promote responsible disposal of e-waste and reduce the environmental impact of the vaping industry.

Our goal is to create a holistic solution that addresses the challenges of convenience and environmental sustainability in the vaping industry.

Information Architecture

Login/Signup: This feature would allow users to create an account and log in to the app using their State ID verification. This would help ensure that only users who are of legal age to purchase vape products are able to access the app.

Home/Vape Category: This feature would allow users to browse and select from different categories of vape products, such as e-liquids, vape pens, and mods.

One Tap Order: This feature would enable users to quickly and easily place an order for their chosen vape products with just a single tap on their device.

Delivery Tracking: This feature would allow users to track the status of their vape delivery in real-time, so they know exactly when to expect their order to arrive.

Payment Options: This feature would enable users to choose from a variety of payment options, such as credit card, debit card, or electronic payment, to securely and conveniently pay for their vape order.

Order History: This feature would enable users to view a history of their past orders, so they can easily reorder their favorite vape products or see what they have ordered in the past.
